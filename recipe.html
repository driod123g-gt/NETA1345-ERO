Easy-Bake Brownies

These brownies make for a very good, low effort, timeless snack to bring at any function. Do not forget to cover if you need to store them.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Betty Crocker Milk Chocolate Brownie Mix
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1/2 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 3 Eggs

    • Buy Ingredients Online at Walmart.ca


    Detail Information
    Prep Time 15 Min
    Bake Time 20 - 25 Minutes
    Total Time 35 - 40 Minutes
    Servings Up To You
    Difficulty Easy
    Oven Temp 350℉(175℃)

    Instructions

    1. Pre-heat oven to 350°F
    2. In a mixing bowl, combine the brownie mix, vegetable oil, eggs, and water. Mix until well combined.
    3. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly.
    4. Bake in the oven for 25 - 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
    5. Allow the brownies to cool before cutting into squares.
    6. ENJOY! :)
